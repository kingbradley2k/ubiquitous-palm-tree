Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

How long does a typical device repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Simple repairs like screen replacements can often be done same-day, while more complex issues may take 2-3 days. We always provide an estimated timeframe after diagnosis.
Do you provide warranty on repairs?
Yes! All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering the specific repair work performed. We also use high-quality replacement parts that come with manufacturer warranties.
What cyber services do you offer?
Our cyber services include printing and document services, data backup and recovery, network setup and security, digital transformation consulting, and ongoing IT support for businesses of all sizes.
Can I trade in my old device?
Absolutely! We accept trade-ins for many electronic devices. The trade-in value depends on the device condition, model, and current market value. Visit our store for a free evaluation.
